Ventura City Council to Hold Closed Session and Regular Meeting

Tuesday, June 2, 2026 at 5:00 PM

City Council Chambers

The Ventura City Council will convene for a closed session and regular meeting on June 2, 2026, starting at 5:00 PM at the City Council Chambers located at 501 Poli St., Ventura, CA 93001. The council typically meets twice a month on Tuesdays. For more information, visit the city calendar at https://www.cityofventura.ca.gov/calendar.aspx?EID=6198.
