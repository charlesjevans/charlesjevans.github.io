About Ventura Buzz

Ventura Buzz is an independent local publication providing real-time information about public meetings and local government events in Ventura, California.

Our purpose is to give residents, business owners, and engaged community members a clear, timely view of what local government is doing — so they can show up, stay informed, or simply know what's happening downtown.

What we do

We track and publish upcoming Ventura public meetings, city council sessions, subcommittee hearings, and official city events. Content is curated, locally focused, and updated hourly.

What we don't do

We do not publish advertisements, sponsored content, opinion pieces, or events outside Ventura. We do not editorialize. We report what is scheduled, where, and when.

Who we are

Ventura Buzz is an independent publication with no affiliation with the City of Ventura or any government body. We are accountable only to our readers.
