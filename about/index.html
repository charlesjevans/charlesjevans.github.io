About Ventura Buzz

Ventura Buzz is an independent local guide to what’s happening now in Ventura, built to help residents, visitors, and downtown passersby quickly see what’s going on nearby, what’s coming up next, and where to look for timely local information as new sources are added over time.

Editorial standards

Ventura Buzz pulls from a growing mix of local sources to help people see what’s happening in Ventura. We prioritize information that is local, timely, and useful, and we update listings as details change or become clearer.

AI disclosure

Ventura Buzz uses AI to help organize and prepare some information that appears on this site. We aim to keep the site clear, timely, and useful, and we continue refining how information is presented as the project grows.

Contact

If you spot an error, have a suggestion, or want to share feedback, contact us at editor@venturabuzz.com.
