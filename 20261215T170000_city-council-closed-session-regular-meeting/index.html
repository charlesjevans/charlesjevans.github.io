Ventura City Council to Hold Closed Session and Regular Meeting

Tuesday, December 15, 2026 at 5:00 PM

City Council Chambers

The Ventura City Council will hold a closed session followed by a regular meeting on December 15, 2026, starting at 5:00 PM. The meeting will take place in the City Council Chambers located at 501 Poli St., Ventura, CA 93001. The council typically meets twice a month on Tuesdays at this time.
