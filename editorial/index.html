Editorial Standards

Sources

Ventura Buzz pulls from a growing mix of local sources to help people see what’s happening in Ventura. We aim to publish timely, useful information about nearby events, activities, and public happenings.

Inclusion criteria

We prioritize items that are local, time-specific, and useful to people who are in Ventura or planning to be nearby. We may leave out listings that are too vague, outdated, duplicated, or not clearly relevant to the area.

Update frequency

The site is updated regularly as new information becomes available and new sources are added. Some listings may change over time as details are clarified, updated, or removed.

Corrections

If you spot an error or outdated listing, please contact us. We want Ventura Buzz to be useful and trustworthy, and we update information when we learn something is wrong or incomplete.

Independence

Ventura Buzz is an independent local project. Inclusion on the site does not imply endorsement, partnership, or official affiliation with any venue, organization, or public agency.
