Ventura City Council to Hold Closed Session and Regular Meeting

Tuesday, May 19, 2026 at 5:00 PM

City Council Chambers

The Ventura City Council will convene for a closed session and regular meeting on May 19, 2026, starting at 5:00 PM at the City Council Chambers, 501 Poli St., Ventura, CA 93001. The council typically meets twice a month on Tuesdays. The agenda for this meeting will include discussions on local development projects and community safety initiatives. For more details, visit the city’s official calendar.
