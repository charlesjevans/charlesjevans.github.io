Ventura City Council to Hold Closed Session and Regular Meeting

Tuesday, July 14, 2026 at 5:00 PM

City Council Chambers

The Ventura City Council will convene for a closed session and regular meeting on July 14, 2026, starting at 5:00 PM. The meeting will take place in the City Council Chambers located at 501 Poli St., Ventura, CA 93001. The council typically meets twice a month on Tuesdays. For more information, visit the city’s calendar at https://www.cityofventura.ca.gov/calendar.aspx?EID=6202.
