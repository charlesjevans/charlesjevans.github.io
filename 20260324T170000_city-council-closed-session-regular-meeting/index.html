Ventura City Council to Hold Closed Session and Regular Meeting

Tuesday, March 24, 2026 at 5:00 PM

The Ventura City Council will convene for a closed session and regular meeting on March 24, 2026, starting at 5:00 PM. The meeting will take place at 57 Day Rd., Ventura, CA 93003. The council typically meets twice a month on Tuesdays. For more information, visit the city’s calendar at https://www.cityofventura.ca.gov/calendar.aspx?EID=6188.
