City Council Committee to Review Standing Committees

Tuesday, April 21, 2026 at 3:00 PM

Community Meeting Room — 501 Poli St., 2nd Floor

The City Council Committee will hold a meeting on April 21, 2026, from 3:00 PM to 11:59 PM at the Community Meeting Room, located at 501 Poli St., 2nd Floor, Ventura, CA 93001. The agenda includes a review of the city council's standing committees. More information can be found at the city’s official calendar [here](https://www.cityofventura.ca.gov/calendar.aspx?EID=6813).
